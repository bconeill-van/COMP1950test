Dog Park Tips

Some rules for taking your dog to the dog park.

Try to remember these rules if you can - maybe take them with you.

  • When you enter the park automatically take your dog of the leash. Not doing so can change the mentality of the pack and even start a fight
  • Clean up after your dog
  • If your dog is getting picked on this is normal, but it is up to you to draw the line between playing and fighting
  • Have fun with your dog but don't run unless you want to get bit
  • Bring a towel! dogs get dirty and unless you want a muddy car bring a towel!
  • There are usually different parks for big and small dogs make sure that you are in the right one
  • Supervise your dog at all times
  • MAKE SURE THAT YOUR DOG HAS ALL OF ITS SHOTS
